Overview

Future-X is a project at Teckplus Digital Solutions in Nigeria. Teckplus is a young and growing tech company in Lagos that is committed to bringing about transformation through innovations and inventions.

The services they offer include web development, mobile app development, cyber security, IOT and machine learning. The project is a cloud base education platform which is responsive and suitable for different screen sizes.


It has the following features:

  • School management system
  • learning management platform
  • Online exams and CBT tests
  • Artificial intelligence
  • and lots more

Future-X private is another version of Future-X designed for private schools in Nigeria. The goal of the project was to design a version of Future-X that would meet the needs of private schools in Nigeria.


My roles and responsibilities

  • User research
  • wireframing
  • prototyping
  • Coordination of design team members (3 members)
  • Collaboration with frontend and Backend team

The process

Research

I started the design process by doing a user research. I conducted interviews with the users at some of the private schools in Nigeria to know their needs and understand their behaviors and attitudes. How are private schools different from public schools in Nigeria? What do they do differently?

How do they differ in terms of administration? How do students in private schools take classes? What are their major challenges and how can Future-X be of help? These and other questions challenged me to do a research with the goal of understanding the new population and their painpoints.


Insights from research

  • Unlike the public schools in Nigeria which is controlled at the state, district and school level, private schools do have a headquarter which controls other school branches.

  • Most teachers in private schools do more tasks than their colleagues in the public schools because private schools don't employ enough staffs.

  • Most of the interviewed proprietors had problems monitoring the activities of their school branches from the headquarter.

  • Private schools use tedious means to send bulk messages to students' parent.

I created a user persona for the project to guide the design process. I analyzed the data obtained from the research together with my team to identify user pain points and we deliberated on multiple ways of solving the problems. Through the use of scenarios, I was able to narrow down the solutions . I created user personas and then use figma for the wireframing process. The design system of the company was used in creating the high-fidelity prototype for this project.

User testing

The design was tested by a group of users. Initially, some of our users had issues with the excel sheet format that was used for bulk uploads. This was redesigned to make the process easier.


Lessons learned

This project showed me the importance of a solid user research in user experience design. When one is creating a new version of an existing app for another user group, one might be tempted to think that the same findings for the first user group will work for the new users. This often lead to the creation of products that do not solve the users' problem. This project clearly show my how a good user research can help in creating products that meet users' need.
