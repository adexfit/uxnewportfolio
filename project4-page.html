Project scope and rationale

This project was done for Gladmak International corporation. Gladmak International Corporation is a USA based corporation that engages in trading, acquisition, brokerage, of bioenergy products, and sale of biomass and agro biomass products for power production, Energy generation and heating purposes.

Gladmak also pursues her interest in renewable energy and takes touchable action to produce clean, low carbon emission and green fuels, and transform biomass and agricultural waste into renewable energy, heat, and electricity, right where it's needed.

The goal of the project was to create a responsive business website for Gladmak that would:

  • increase brand awareness
  • establish credibility and build trust with customers
  • help in conveying information to users
  • increase sales and revenue
  • improve customer experience

This project lasted for 3 weeks and I was the only designer who worked on it.


My roles and responsibilities

  • Information architecture
  • wireframing
  • prototyping
  • Usability testing

The process

This project started with a brief from Gladmak which included all the necessary information like the name of the company, about the company, project goals, Key dates & deadlines, audience for project and deliverable outcomes e.t.c.

From the brief, I was able to extract the product information, business goals, and information about the targeted audience, which helped in the creation of a user persona.

After going through the user requirements and user pain points highlighted, I ideated on the possible means of solving the problem and narrow down my solution using scenarios. I created the wireframe on Figma which was reviewed by the client and was later developed into a high fidelity prototype on Figma.

User testing

The low fidelity wireframe was first tested with some group of users that had the attributes in our user persona before it was developed into a hight-fidelity mockup that was later given to the developement team. Most of the users were able to easily navigate the app with ease during the user testing process.
