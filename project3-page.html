REAL ESTATE APP REDESIGN

  1. Home
  2. REAL ESTATE APP REDESIGN

Overview

The goal of this project was to design a mobile application that connects tenants to landlord so that tenants in Nigeria can easily find houses of their choice.

This project lasted for 6 months and I was the only product designer who participated in the redesign.


Users and audience

The mobile app was designed for tenants and landlords in Nigeria.


The problems

The initial design of this app had so many flaws which necessitated a redesign to address the usability issues. When I picked up the challenge to redesign the app for the company, I conducted a usability testing on the existing design and discovered the following pain points and flaws in the design:

  • The onboarding pages confused most of the users. Most of the users were trying to perform actions on the onboarding pages thinking that they were already in the main part of the app. This is because the information on the onboarding pages was not properly conveyed to the users.

  • There are no link/buttons to take users to some important pages. An example of this is the main page where users search for houses. After users have been onboarded and shown the available houses, there is no link to take them back to this important page to search for houses again.

  • Some important user flows were missing.

  • Authentication was stressful for the users. The app had 4 different login pages for each of the stakeholders which made authentication stressful for stakeholders with overlapping roles. They have to unnecessarily sign in and out to perform their duties.

Aside these problems, I also noted the following problems:
  • No design system
  • No user persona

My roles and responsibilities

  • User research
  • wireframing
  • prototyping
  • Usability testing

The process

I used the user centered design approach and the design thinking process of the Stanford school of design for this project.



I interviewed the important stakeholders in the company to gather the product information and the business goals. I conducted a user research to understand the behavior and the thinking pattern of the targeted users.

After analyzing the research, I created user persona for the project and brainstorm on the user pain points. Through the use of scenarios, I was able to arrive at a better way of solving the problem.

User persona


I created a design system for the project which was initially not done. This helped in ensuring consistency and made it easy for me to easily change things when necessary.

Through the knowledge of " conditional rendering" in react, I redesigned the app to work correctly for all the stakeholders using a simple logic and without creating 4 different authentications. The logic did not only save the users the stress of switching from one dashboard to another, it also saved the developers from the stress of designing four different authentications.

I also redesign the onboarding pages of the mobile app which was initially misleading the users during user testing. It was redesigned into a simple informative and easy-to-use page that truly onboard the users.

I created low-fidelity wireframes using Figma. This was developed into a high-fidelity mockup upon satisfactory user testing results.
